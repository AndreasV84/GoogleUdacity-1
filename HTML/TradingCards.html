Spongemock

The image of SpongeBob used for this series comes from the episode “Little Yellow Book,” which first aired on November 25th, 2012.

  • Age 7 Months
  • Type Image

Spongemock,refers to an image macro featuring cartoon character SpongeBob SquarePants in which people use a picture to indicate a mocking tone towards an opinion or point of view.

Cool Story Bro

“Cool Story, Bro” is a catchphrase often seen in image macros as a sarcastic response in comments that are deemed boring, pointless or too long to read.

  • Age 9 Years
  • Type Image
  • Still Active Sometimes
  • Location of Origin 4chan /v/

The phrase “cool story" used as a sarcastic response to a narrative was first seen in film Zoolander where character Hansel tells a long winded story about a drug-induced hallucination.

LOLcats

The LOLcat meme gained much of its traction through the weekly ritual of Caturday on 4chan and I Can Has Cheezburger.

  • Age of Meme 9 years+
  • Type of Meme Image
  • Still Active Yes
  • Location of Origin 4chan

LOLcats originated on the imageboard 4chan sometime in 2005, when an anonymous user submitted a picture of a relaxed cat waiting for a forum tradition.. “Caturday.”
